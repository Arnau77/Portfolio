Projects
Big Projects
Mandalorian: Ashes of the Empire

Mandalorian: Ashes of the Empire is a roguelike game in 3D in which you control Mando and fight against the remnants of the Empire. This game was done in 4 months by all the third-year students (32) of the degree of Design and Development of Video Games in the CITM (UPC) in Terrassa. UI, save system, QA and bug fixing are among the things that I worked on

Mythology Parade

Mythology Parade is an RTS game in 2D in which you try to impose your religion (either Greek or Norse) on the other one. This game was done in 4 months by a team of 8 second-year students of the degree of Design and Development of Video Games in the CITM (UPC) in Terrassa. UI, units, bug fixing and camera are among the things that I worked on

Schrodinger Quest

Schrodinger Quest is a platformer in 2D with two levels, in which you advance by moving, jumping and changing your tangibility. This game was done in 4 months by a team of 2 second-year students of the degree of Design and Development of Video Games in the CITM (UPC) in Terrassa. UI, state machines, enemies and changes between scenes are among the things that I worked on

Samurai Shodown

Samurai Shodown is a recreation of the fighting game in 2D created by SNK in which you can fight as Ukyo. This game was done in 4 months by a team of 4 first-year students of the degree of Design and Development of Video Games in the CITM (UPC) in Terrassa. UI, state machines, animation of characters and bug fixing are among the things that I worked on
